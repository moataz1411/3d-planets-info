Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. It is known for its reddish appearance because the red color of iron on its surface. Also, it is one of the most explored planets.

Jupiter

jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system and contains a famous storm known as (The Great Red Spot).

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its spectacular ring system and is the second largest planet in our solar system after jupiter.

Uranus
